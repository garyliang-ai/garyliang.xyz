**Hi, I'm Gary, born and raised in Sydney, Australia.**

**I run a startup called [Bloom AI](https://bloom.study/?utm_source=garyliangxyz&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=garyliangxyz)**, an AI tutor platform for schools and universities. I write about [AI in education on Substack](https://garyliang.substack.com/). I also provide [strategy consulting to educational institutions and startups](https://bloom.study/consulting/?utm_source=garyliangxyz&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=garyliangxyz).

**I have over a decade of experience as an educator**, both at a high school and university level. I teach economics and mathematics at UNSW and the University of Sydney. I previously started and operated a personalised high school tutoring company. I'm gradually publishing my [high school notes online](https://glnotes.com/?utm_source=garyliangxyz&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=garyliangxyz) and have authored an [economics workbook for Year 12 students](https://www.amazon.com.au/dp/B0BCSBGPLH).

**I was a management consultant** at [McKinsey & Company](https://mckinsey.com). I was a Fellow at the [McKinsey Global Institute](https://www.mckinsey.com/mgi/overview) thinktank, where I did research on the [Future of Asian consumers](https://www.mckinsey.com/featured-insights/asia-pacific/beyond-income-redrawing-asias-consumer-map), [global flows](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/strategy-and-corporate-finance/our-insights/global-flows-the-ties-that-bind-in-an-interconnected-world) and the [African economy](https://www.mckinsey.com/mgi/our-research/reimagining-economic-growth-in-africa-turning-diversity-into-opportunity). I've also worked at a [no-code automation startup](https://www.solvexia.com/), and had stints at Goldman Sachs and a high frequency trading firm.

**I studied economics and pure mathematics at UNSW and Wharton**. I wrote my [Honours thesis in graph theory](/honours/), which resulted in a published [paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.07350). I've previously been an academic researcher in economics (game theory and industrial organisation), mathematics (combinatorics and graph theory) and computer science (blockchain and data provenance). My [Erdős number](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erd%C5%91s_number) is 3.
