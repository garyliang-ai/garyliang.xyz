I'm Gary: founder, educator, and advisor. Born and raised in Sydney, based in New York.

## Now

Building [Bloom AI](https://bloom.study/?utm_source=garyliangxyz&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=garyliangxyz), AI built for learning, rather than just answers. Writing about [AI in education](https://garyliang.substack.com/) on Substack. 

## Before

Teaching economics and mathematics at UNSW and the University of Sydney. Founding a high school tutoring centre. Management consulting at [McKinsey](https://mckinsey.com), including a fellowship at the McKinsey Global Institute thinktank. Before that: Goldman Sachs, IMC, and a [no-code automation startup](https://www.solvexia.com/).

Studied economics and pure mathematics at UNSW and Wharton. My [Honours thesis](/honours/) in graph theory became a [published paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.07350). My [Erdős number](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erd%C5%91s_number) is 3.
