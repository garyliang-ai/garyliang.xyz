### Research

My current research is in AI and education. I've previously done research in economics (game theory and industrial organisation), mathematics (combinatorics and graph theory) and computer science (blockchain and data provenance).

My [Honours thesis](/honours/) on spanning trees in random hypergraphs became a [published paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.07350). You can read the [thesis itself](/files/thesis.pdf) or an [accessible overview](/honours/). My [Erdős number](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erd%C5%91s_number) is 3 via [Professor Catherine Greenhill](https://web.maths.unsw.edu.au/~csg/).
